Project Overview


Welcome to Team Alpha! We are otherwise known as the solutionists and here is why! Ever wonder if where you go on vacation or where you want to live in the world is prone to natural disasters such as Earthquakes?

We are here to demonstrate the visualizations that clearly depict years of earthquake data from the USGS.gov site in order to show a correlation or a trend of which can ultimately determine the predictability or likelihood of and earthquake occurring in a specific location of your choosing based on its occurrence throughout the years.

Through the usage of Python, JavaScript, D3, Leaftlet, Tableau and MongoDB Atlas we are able to produce interactive geo-graphical visualizations, multifunctional diagrams and data analysis to help answer some of those concerns.

Please check out the Data visualization option on the navbar!!!
